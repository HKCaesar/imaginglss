imaginglss.analysis.targetselection module¶

Target Selection

https://desi.lbl.gov/trac/wiki/TargetSelection

A collection of helpful (static) methods to check whether an object’s flux passes a given selection criterion (e.g. LRG, ELG or QSO).

These cuts assume we are passed the extinction-corrected fluxes (flux/mw_transmission) and are taken from:

https://desi.lbl.gov/trac/wiki/TargetSelection
imaginglss.analysis.targetselection.LRG = ((BRICK_PRIMARY != 0) & ((DECAM_FLUX.T[2] / DECAM_MW_TRANSMISSION.T[2]) > 0.6309573444801932) & ((DECAM_FLUX.T[4] / DECAM_MW_TRANSMISSION.T[4]) > 5.970352865838375) & ((WISE_FLUX.T[0] / WISE_MW_TRANSMISSION.T[0]) > 18.197008586099805) & ((DECAM_FLUX.T[4] / DECAM_MW_TRANSMISSION.T[4]) > ((DECAM_FLUX.T[2] / DECAM_MW_TRANSMISSION.T[2]) * 4.36515832240166)) & (((WISE_FLUX.T[0] / WISE_MW_TRANSMISSION.T[0]) * ((DECAM_FLUX.T[2] / DECAM_MW_TRANSMISSION.T[2]) ** 0.33000000000000007)) > (((DECAM_FLUX.T[4] / DECAM_MW_TRANSMISSION.T[4]) ** 1.33) * 0.737904230129101)))¶
